## ⚠️ UPPA COMPLIANCE (Texas Law)

**Texas contractors CANNOT act as public adjusters.**

### Language Rules for Insurance Content:

| ✅ COMPLIANT | ❌ ILLEGAL |
|-------------|-----------|
| "We document damage for your insurance claim" | "We negotiate with your insurance" |
| "Free inspection for insurance purposes" | "We handle your claim for you" |
| "We provide detailed damage reports" | "We'll get you maximum payout" |
| "We work with insurance companies" | "We adjust insurance claims" |
| "Insurance claim documentation assistance" | "We'll fight the insurance company" |

**Source:** Texas Insurance Code - Unlicensed Practice of Public Adjusting (UPPA)
