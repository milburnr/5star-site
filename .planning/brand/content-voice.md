# Content Voice Guide

Reference this in phases that create or modify page content (Phases 3, 4, 6, 8).

## Tone

- Professional but approachable — local expert, not corporate chain
- Storm-response focused — urgency without fear-mongering
- Specific and factual — concrete details over empty claims
- Residential-first — lead with homeowner concerns, commercial secondary

## Good vs Bad Patterns

### Meta Descriptions

| Bad | Good |
|-----|------|
| "Looking for roofing services in Amarillo?" | "Get your Amarillo roof inspected within 24 hours after hail storms" |
| "We offer quality roofing at affordable prices" | "Licensed Amarillo roofer specializing in hail damage repair since 2016" |
| "Call us today for a free estimate!" | "Free storm damage inspections for Amarillo homeowners — call (806) 622-6041" |

### H1/H2 Headings

| Bad | Good |
|-----|------|
| "Our Roofing Services" | "Roof Repair in Amarillo, TX" |
| "Why Choose Us" | "How Long Does Hail Damage Repair Take in Amarillo?" |
| "About Our Company" | "What Does a Roof Inspection Cover?" |

Use answer-first H2 structure: frame H2s as actual homeowner questions.

### Page Content

| Bad | Good |
|-----|------|
| "We are the leading roofing company..." | "Amarillo averages 3-4 major hail storms per year..." |
| "Quality service, affordable prices" | "We use GAF Timberline HDZ shingles rated for 130 mph winds" |
| Same FAQ on every page | City-specific: "How does Amarillo's clay soil affect roof foundations?" |
| "Call us today!" | "Amarillo homeowners: Free roof inspection after every storm" |

### CTAs

- Always include local context
- Include phone number inline where natural
- Residential-first framing

| Bad | Good |
|-----|------|
| "Contact Us" | "Schedule Your Free Amarillo Roof Inspection" |
| "Call Now" | "Call (806) 622-6041 — Same-Day Storm Response" |
| "Get Started" | "Get Your Free Hail Damage Assessment" |

## City-Specific Content Requirements

Every city page must include at least 3 genuinely city-specific elements:
- Local weather data relevant to roofing (hail frequency, wind speeds, temperature extremes)
- Neighborhood or area names within the city
- City-specific building codes, HOA requirements, or permit info
- Local landmarks or geographic references for context
- City-specific FAQ questions

Never: Generic content with city name swapped in. Every city page should read as if written by someone who knows the city.

## FAQ Standards

**Service pages (6-7 questions):**
1. How much does [service] cost in [city]?
2. How long does [service] take?
3. Does insurance cover [service]?
4. What are signs I need [service]?
5. Can I do [service] myself or hire a professional?
6. How do I choose a [service] contractor in [city]?
7. What materials do you use for [service]?

**City pages (6 questions):**
1. Do you serve [city] and surrounding areas?
2. What roofing services do you offer in [city]?
3. How quickly can you respond to storm damage in [city]?
4. Are you licensed and insured in [city]?
5. What makes [city] roofing different? (climate, codes)
6. Do you work with insurance adjusters in [city]?

These are templates — adapt to each page's specific context. Never copy verbatim across pages.
