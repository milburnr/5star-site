================================================================================
BEST PRACTICES SUMMARY
================================================================================

QUESTIONS:
  - 5-15 words each
  - Start with: What, How, Why, Can, Do, Should
  - Address customer pain points
  - Natural, conversational tone

ANSWERS:
  - 2-4 sentences
  - Include specific details (materials, costs, timeframes)
  - Use local references (city names, climate factors)
  - Educational, not promotional
  - Naturally answer the question

QUANTITY:
  - Minimum: 3 Q&A pairs
  - Optimal: 4-5 Q&A pairs
  - Maximum: 8-10 Q&A pairs
  - Only include relevant topics

COMMON ROOFING TOPICS:
  - Material selection and benefits
  - Lifespan and durability
  - Costs and pricing
  - Climate/weather impact
  - Insurance discounts
  - Maintenance requirements
  - Emergency response
  - Warranty coverage
